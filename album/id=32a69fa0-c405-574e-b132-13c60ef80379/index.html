Origins

2018

On July 12, 2018, Imagine Dragons announced a new single titled "Natural" on Twitter. The song was released on July 17, 2018. The song is confirmed to be the anthem for the 2018 ESPN College Football season. On September 18, 2018, Imagine Dragons announced a new single titled "Zero", which was released on September 19, 2018. It will be used in the end credits of the upcoming Walt Disney Animation Studios film Ralph Breaks the Internet.

On October 3, 2018, Imagine Dragons announced their fourth studio album, Origins, which was released on November 9, 2018. "Natural" and "Zero" serve as the lead singles off the album. The band has described this album as a sister album to their previous work Evolve. On October 31, 2018, Imagine Dragons released their third single off the album, "Machine". Lastly, on November 6, 2018, Imagine Dragons released their fourth single of the album, "Bad Liar".
